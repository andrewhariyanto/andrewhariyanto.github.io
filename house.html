Game Description

Created using Unity3D and assets from the Unity Store, explore the mysterious and magical mansion that withstood the test of time. Gaze at the majestic mountains and the expansive seas. Unravel the story behind this mansion and leave your mark in the level!

Contributions and What I Learned

  • Scripted player controls.
  • Sculpted and painted terrain.
  • Used downloadable assets to design the interior and exterior of the house.
  • Modified those assets to fit the setting by designing my own textures and materials
  • Implemented proper lightmaps
  • Manipulated lighting and sounds
  • Created the trailer using Lightworks.
